      Methodology

      This calculator estimates CRF allocations using the IRC's standard per-client cost of $47.34, representing the average direct program cost per person served across IRC emergency responses over 6 months. The IRC target population defaults to 10% of the affected population, adjustable based on operational access and feasibility.

      Coverage options represent the percentage of the total affected population that CRF funds directly, from 1% up to 3% of affected people. The remaining gap represents the external fundraising required to reach the IRC's 10% response goal. All figures are rounded to the nearest $25,000.
