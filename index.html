Methodology

This calculator estimates CRF funding allocations using the IRC's standard per-client cost of $47.34, representing the average direct program cost per person served across IRC emergency responses. The IRC target population defaults to 10% of the affected population, adjustable based on operational access and feasibility.

Funding ratios show what IRC commits from its Crisis Response Fund relative to total need. The recommended target is $1 in every $3 — for every $3 of total funding needed, IRC leads with $1 from CRF. This signals serious institutional investment and strengthens the case for external donor funding to close the gap. All figures are rounded to the nearest $100.
