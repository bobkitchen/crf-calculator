Methodology

This calculator estimates funding allocations using the IRC's standard per-client cost of $47.34, representing the average direct program cost per person served across IRC emergency responses. Two calculation methods are available: Target Population lets you specify the number of people to serve (defaulting to 10% of the affected population), while CRF Allocation % calculates based on 1–5% of the total affected population. All figures are rounded to the nearest $100.
